Research Overview

I work on Applied Data Science, Data Engineering for AI and Business, Privacy Economics, AI Ethics. My works focus on practical problem solving, risk tracking and system-level improvement for human good. I am also interested in the misuse of mass media and its psychological impact, especially new ways of cyber-social attacks through a personalized linguistic environment. I work to figure out effective solutions for potential victims, both on the computer science side and the psychological side.
